MyAR

Meet MyAR, an app that uses the 5G technology and Augmented Reality (AR) to render 4D images.
Yeah you read that right, all from the comfort of your phone.

With MyAR app, you experience high resolution holographic effects and get a clear voice recognition.

FEATURES TO EXPECT

4D Holographic Image
Ever missed the feeling of speaking to a loved one, relative or colleague in person as though they were in a room with you?

Search no further, with MyAR you get a 4D image rendering of your loved one with resolutions up to 4K with no special hardware.

The best part is that you won't need your phone's processor to achieve this feat, we have dedicated algorithm and servers that do that for you.

All you need to do is just have a relaxed interaction and enjoy your time wiith any of your contacts from the comfort of your phone
Easy Setup
Are you tired of long and stressfull sign up where sometimes you're even asked even up to place of birth?. MyAR things will be so much easier,no Email sign up or registration. Simply download the app and put your phone number. You're ready to be immersed in 4D image calls
Real Time Sync
With the aid of the 5G technology integration, you don't have to worry about out of sync conversations or buffering or stuttering. You get a smooth and clear image and audio
Multiple Connections
MyAR would not be limited to only a one-on-one call but can have up to 20 persons simultaneously, so wether you want to have a meeting with multiple contact at the same time, MyAR is capable of handling up to 20 user requests at the same time and this is would be just the beginning
Available For Android and IOS
We are well aware of the limitations of apps availablity due to OS differences so we are making sure no party would be left out, so Regardless of the device type, MyAR will be available for use in both the IOS and Andriod operating system
Free Headphones
First 100 users to donwload the app get's a free 7.1 surround sound headphones shipped to their address at no cost. And with this headphones you are sure of clear and audible sounds as well as advance noise reduction, of cause all ear phones are compatible so you don't have to worry about buying headphones out of your budget.
